Good Sites for your Old Macs

Some awesome pages to get your old Mac up and running!

Macintosh Garden - A good resource for downloading old apps and games Macintosh Repository - Another good resource for old apps and games Apple Fool - Information about Macs and Mac Networking MacFixer - A useful site for downloading software for computers old as the Apple II! FrogFind - A search engine based on DuckDuckGo that converts all search results and webpages into basic HTML results. iFixIt - A huge site full of computer repair guides (may not load on an old mac.)
